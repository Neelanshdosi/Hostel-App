NMIMS Hostel App is a dedicated hostel management and student utility application built to simplify everyday hostel life for NMIMS residents and improve coordination between students and hostel authorities. The app provides a single digital platform where students can access hostel-related services quickly and transparently instead of relying on informal messaging or physical notice boards.
